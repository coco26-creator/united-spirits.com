Tasting Notes

This exceptional 18-year-old bourbon presents a deep mahogany color with ruby highlights. The nose is rich and inviting, offering aromas of caramel, vanilla bean, toasted oak, and subtle hints of dried fruit and baking spices. On the palate, it delivers a luxurious mouthfeel with flavors of butterscotch, dark chocolate, and gentle tobacco notes. The finish is remarkably smooth and long-lasting, with warming notes of oak and a touch of cinnamon.

Origin & Distillation

Crafted at a historic distillery in Kentucky, this premium bourbon is made from a carefully balanced mash bill of corn, rye, and malted barley. Aged for 18 years in new charred American oak barrels, it develops exceptional depth and complexity. The barrels are stored in the center of the rickhouse where temperature variations are most moderate, allowing for a slow, even maturation process that produces a remarkably smooth spirit. Each bottle is hand-selected by the master distiller for its exceptional quality.

Gift Presentation & Serving

Presented in an elegant wooden gift box with satin lining, this bourbon makes an exceptional gift for special occasions. The box includes two crystal whiskey glasses and a tasting guide. Best enjoyed neat at room temperature to appreciate its full complexity, though it also makes an outstanding base for classic cocktails like the Old Fashioned or Manhattan. For optimal tasting experience, allow the bourbon to breathe for 5-10 minutes after pouring to fully appreciate its nuanced aromas and flavors.
