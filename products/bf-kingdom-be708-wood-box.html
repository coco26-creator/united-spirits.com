Tasting Notes

This exceptional blended whiskey presents a deep mahogany color with ruby highlights. The nose is remarkably complex, offering a symphony of dried fruits, caramel, toasted oak, and subtle hints of vanilla and baking spices. On the palate, it delivers a luxurious mouthfeel with flavors of dark chocolate, honey, and gentle spice. The finish is exceptionally long and warming, with lingering notes of oak and citrus zest. The 47.08% ABV provides remarkable depth and intensity while maintaining perfect balance and smoothness.

Craftsmanship & Aging

BF Kingdom BE708 represents the pinnacle of blending artistry, combining selected single malts aged between 15-25 years with exceptional grain whiskeys. Each component is carefully chosen from distilleries across Scotland, with a focus on Speyside and Highland malts for their fruity complexity and Islay components for subtle smoky depth. The blend is married in first-fill ex-sherry casks for 12 months, allowing the components to integrate while absorbing additional layers of flavor from the wood. The precise 47.08% ABV was selected after extensive tasting sessions to showcase the blend's exceptional complexity while maintaining approachability.

Wooden Presentation & Experience

Housed in a handcrafted wooden box made from sustainable oak, this whiskey set represents the ultimate in luxury presentation. The box features intricate detailing and a magnetic closure, designed to be kept as a premium storage container long after the bottle is enjoyed. Inside, the bottle sits nestled in custom-cut foam with a booklet detailing the whiskey's provenance and tasting notes. For the optimal experience, serve at room temperature in a tulip-shaped whiskey glass. Allow it to breathe for 10-15 minutes after pouring to fully appreciate its nuanced aromas. This whiskey can be enjoyed neat, with a drop of spring water, or with a single large ice cube for a slightly chilled experience. It makes an exceptional gift for the most discerning whiskey connoisseurs.
