Red Wine

Bernard Magrez Le Symbole 2020

Bordeaux, France

Modern Bordeaux with dark fruit aromas, hints of vanilla and spice. Smooth and well-structured with a lingering finish. Perfect for pairing with beef or lamb.

← Back to Wines

Tasting Notes

The 2020 vintage offers a deep, intense ruby color with garnet reflections. The nose is complex and expressive, revealing aromas of ripe blackcurrant, dark cherry, and subtle notes of vanilla, tobacco, and spice. On the palate, it is full-bodied and well-structured, with smooth tannins and a refreshing acidity that leads to a long, elegant finish with lingering notes of dark fruit and oak.

Origin & Terroir

Crafted by the legendary Bernard Magrez, this wine is sourced from select vineyards in the prestigious Bordeaux region. The grapes are grown in gravelly soils with excellent drainage, which is ideal for producing concentrated, high-quality fruit. The region’s maritime climate, with warm summers and mild winters, allows the grapes to achieve optimal ripeness while retaining their natural acidity.

Food Pairing

This wine is a perfect companion to fine dining, pairing beautifully with dishes such as beef Wellington, roasted lamb, or duck confit. It also complements aged cheeses like Comté or Gruyère. For the best experience, decant for 1-2 hours and serve at 18°C (64°F) in a large Bordeaux glass.
