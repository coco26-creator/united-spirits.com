Scotch

Sunny Land Single Malt S25

25-Year-Old • Scotland

Exceptional 25-year-old single malt Scotch with notes of dried fruit, honey, and subtle oak. Complex and refined with a smooth, lingering finish. Perfect for special occasions.

← Back to Whiskey

Tasting Notes

This exceptional 25-year-old single malt presents a deep amber color with golden highlights. The nose is rich and complex, offering aromas of dried fruits, honey, toasted oak, and subtle hints of vanilla and spice. On the palate, it delivers a smooth, velvety texture with flavors of dark chocolate, caramelized apples, and gentle peat smoke. The finish is exceptionally long and warming, with lingering notes of oak and citrus zest.

Origin & Distillation

Crafted at a historic distillery in the Speyside region of Scotland, this single malt was carefully matured in hand-selected oak casks for 25 years. The distillery's unique water source from the nearby river and traditional copper pot stills contribute to its distinctive character. Each cask was monitored by master blenders who determined the perfect moment for bottling to capture the peak expression of this exceptional spirit.

Serving Recommendations

Best enjoyed neat in a Glencairn glass at room temperature to fully appreciate its complex aromas. For a different experience, add a few drops of spring water to open up additional flavor notes. This single malt pairs beautifully with dark chocolate, dried fruits, or a quality cigar. Store the bottle upright in a cool, dark place to preserve its quality.
