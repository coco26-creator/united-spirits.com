Tasting Notes

This premium blended whiskey presents a rich golden amber color with copper highlights. The nose is complex and inviting, offering a beautiful harmony of dried fruits, honey, toasted oak, and subtle floral notes. On the palate, it delivers a silky texture with flavors of caramelized apples, vanilla, and gentle spice. The finish is remarkably smooth and long-lasting, with lingering notes of citrus zest and warm baking spices. The 40.8% ABV provides exceptional balance between intensity and smoothness.

Blend Composition & Craftsmanship

BF Kingdom BE408 is a masterful blend of carefully selected single malts and grain whiskeys, each aged for a minimum of 12 years. The blend features components from Speyside, Highland, and Islay distilleries, creating a harmonious balance of fruity sweetness, gentle smoke, and rich oak character. Each component is chosen by the master blender for its distinctive qualities, then married together in ex-sherry casks for an additional 6 months to create perfect integration. The precise 40.8% ABV was selected after extensive tasting to showcase the blend's complexity while maintaining approachability.

Gift Presentation & Experience

Presented in an elegant wooden gift box with satin lining, this whiskey set includes the bottle, two crystal whiskey glasses, a tasting guide, and a pipette for precise water addition. The box itself is designed for reuse as a premium storage container. For the optimal tasting experience, allow the whiskey to breathe for 5-10 minutes after pouring. It can be enjoyed neat, with a drop of spring water to open up additional flavor dimensions, or with a single large ice cube for a slightly chilled experience. This whiskey makes an exceptional gift for special occasions or for building your own premium collection.
