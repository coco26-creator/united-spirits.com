Red Wine

Corazón de Lolita 2020

Spain

Aromatic red wine with flavors of ripe berries, vanilla, and subtle spice. Medium-bodied with soft tannins. Pairs perfectly with tapas and grilled dishes.

← Back to Wines

Tasting Notes

The 2020 vintage offers a bright, ruby-red color with vibrant purple hues. The nose is expressive and inviting, presenting aromas of ripe blackberry, dark cherry, and subtle notes of vanilla and cinnamon. On the palate, it is medium-bodied with soft, supple tannins and a refreshing acidity that leads to a smooth, lingering finish with hints of spice and oak.

Origin & Terroir

Produced in the heart of Spain's wine regions, this wine reflects the unique characteristics of the local terroir. The vineyards benefit from a Mediterranean climate with warm, dry summers and mild winters, allowing the grapes to develop intense flavors while maintaining their natural acidity. The soils, composed of limestone and clay, contribute to the wine's structure and mineral complexity.

Food Pairing

This versatile wine pairs beautifully with a variety of Spanish dishes, including paella, chorizo, or tapas platters. It also complements grilled meats, aged cheeses, or even dark chocolate desserts. For the best experience, serve at 16-18°C (60-64°F) in a medium-sized Burgundy glass.
