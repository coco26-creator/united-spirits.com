Tasting Notes

This ready-to-drink red wine offers a vibrant, fruity profile with bright notes of ripe berries and subtle hints of vanilla. The flavor is clean and refreshing, with a balanced sweetness that makes it easy to enjoy. The finish is crisp and clean, leaving a pleasant, lingering taste of fresh fruit without the metallic aftertaste often associated with canned beverages.

Origin & Production

Crafted in Spain, this innovative RTD product combines traditional winemaking with modern convenience. The wine is carefully selected from premium Spanish vineyards, then packaged using specialized canning technology that preserves freshness and flavor. The cans are lined with a protective barrier to prevent any interaction between the wine and the aluminum, ensuring the authentic taste of Spanish wine in a portable format.

Serving Suggestions

Best served chilled at 8-10°C (46-50°F). The convenient 250ml cans are perfect for outdoor events, picnics, festivals, or any occasion where portability matters. Each can provides a single serving of premium red wine without the need for glasses or corkscrews. For the best experience, pour into a glass to fully appreciate the aromas, or enjoy straight from the can for ultimate convenience.
