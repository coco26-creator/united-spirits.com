Single Malt

Sunny Land Single Malt France

France Finish

Distinctive single malt finished in French oak barrels. Features notes of dried fruit, vanilla, and subtle spice. Complex profile with a smooth finish. Best enjoyed neat or with a drop of water.

← Back to Whiskey

Tasting Notes

This unique single malt presents a rich amber color with copper highlights. The nose reveals a beautiful interplay between traditional Scotch characteristics and French oak influences, with aromas of dried apricots, vanilla bean, toasted almonds, and subtle floral notes. On the palate, it delivers a silky texture with flavors of caramelized pears, honey, and gentle oak spice. The finish is elegant and balanced, with lingering notes of citrus and warm baking spices.

Origin & Finishing Process

Initially matured in traditional American oak barrels for 18 years in Scotland, this exceptional single malt undergoes a secondary maturation in carefully selected French oak barrels from the Bordeaux region. The French oak imparts distinctive characteristics that complement the Scotch base, adding complexity and depth. This finishing process is monitored by master blenders who determine the precise duration to achieve perfect balance between the Scotch character and French oak influence.

Serving Recommendations

Best enjoyed in a tulip-shaped whiskey glass at room temperature. Adding a few drops of spring water will open up additional flavor dimensions. This single malt pairs exceptionally well with blue cheeses, dried fruits, or dark chocolate with 70% cocoa. For a special experience, try it alongside a quality cigar with medium intensity. The French oak finish makes it particularly versatile for pairing with both traditional Scotch accompaniments and more delicate flavors.
