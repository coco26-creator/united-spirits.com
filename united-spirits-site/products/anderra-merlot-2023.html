Red Wine

Anderra Merlot 2023

Chile

A smooth and elegant Merlot with notes of ripe plum, blackberry, and subtle oak. Medium-bodied with soft tannins and a lingering finish. Best enjoyed with grilled meats or pasta.

← Back to Wines

Tasting Notes

This 2023 vintage offers a deep ruby color with vibrant purple hues. On the nose, it reveals intense aromas of ripe red fruits — think cherry and raspberry — complemented by subtle hints of vanilla and spice from its oak aging. The palate is smooth and velvety, with well-integrated tannins and a beautifully balanced acidity that leads to a long, satisfying finish.

Origin & Terroir

Sourced from the sun-drenched vineyards of Chile’s Maipo Valley, this Merlot benefits from a unique microclimate. The cool nights and warm days allow the grapes to develop optimal ripeness while preserving their natural acidity. The soil, rich in clay and alluvial deposits, imparts structure and depth to the wine.

Food Pairing

Perfectly paired with red meats, lamb, or hearty pasta dishes. Also excellent with aged cheeses like Gouda or Manchego. Serve at 16-18°C (60-64°F) in a large Bordeaux glass to fully appreciate its complex bouquet.
