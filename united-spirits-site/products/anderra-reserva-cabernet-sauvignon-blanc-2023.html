Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon Blanc 2023

Tasting Notes

A rare and exquisite white wine crafted from Cabernet Sauvignon grapes. Expect a crisp, refreshing profile with vibrant notes of green apple, citrus zest, and subtle floral hints. The finish is clean, aromatic, and beautifully balanced, making it a truly unique experience for the discerning palate.

Origin & Terroir

Sourced from high-altitude vineyards in Chile’s renowned Maipo Valley. The cool nights and sun-drenched days create the perfect conditions for preserving the grape’s natural acidity and developing its complex aromatic character. The grapes are hand-harvested at optimal ripeness to ensure peak flavor.

Production

This innovative wine is produced using a meticulous cold-pressing technique to extract the juice without skin contact, preserving its delicate pale hue and fresh character. Fermented in temperature-controlled stainless steel tanks to retain its vibrant fruit profile, it is then aged for 4 months on fine lees to add subtle texture and complexity.

Serving Suggestion

Serve chilled at 8-10°C (46-50°F) in a white wine glass. Pairs exceptionally well with light seafood dishes, goat cheese, or as an aperitif. Its unique profile also makes it a conversation-starting centerpiece for any dinner party.

  • “Best Innovative White” — Decanter World Wine Awards
