Tasting Notes

This ready-to-drink cocktail offers a vibrant, fruity profile with bright citrus and tropical fruit notes. The flavor is well-balanced with a smooth, refreshing finish that's easy to enjoy. The drink has a light, crisp character that makes it perfect for warm weather or casual gatherings.

Origin & Production

Crafted in Chile, this RTD combines premium ingredients with innovative blending techniques to deliver a consistently delicious experience. The recipe features carefully selected fruit essences and natural flavors, ensuring a premium taste profile that's perfect for any occasion. The product is produced under strict quality control standards.

Food Pairing

This versatile RTD pairs beautifully with a variety of foods, including light appetizers, seafood, salads, and tropical fruit dishes. It's also perfect as a refreshing accompaniment to casual barbecues or outdoor events. For the best experience, serve chilled and enjoy as a standalone drink or in cocktails.
