Private Client Visit

February 2023 • Qingdao Free Trade Zone

Personalized tour and tasting experience for discerning collectors and high-net-worth clients. This exclusive event offered a behind-the-scenes look at our rarest bottles and a curated tasting led by our master sommelier.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

This intimate event welcomed a select group of VIP clients to our private cellar in Qingdao. The evening began with a guided tour of the “Rare Bottle Archive,” where guests viewed bottles dating back decades. The tour was followed by a seated tasting of five ultra-premium expressions, paired with gourmet canapés.

Key Highlights

The highlight was the unveiling of a 50-year-old Scotch, one of only a few bottles remaining in the world. Guests also enjoyed a rare tasting of Honney Conti XO Royal, served in hand-blown crystal glasses. The master sommelier shared fascinating stories about each bottle’s provenance and production, creating a truly memorable experience.

Featured Products

The tasting featured these rare treasures:

  • Sunny Land Single Malt 50-Year-Old - Extremely limited release, highly sought after by collectors
  • Honney Conti XO Royal Decanter - Hand-blown crystal decanter, numbered and signed
  • Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon 1985 - A legendary vintage, considered one of the best of the decade
  • Pre-Prohibition Bourbon - A rare American whiskey from the early 20th century
