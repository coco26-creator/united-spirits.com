Private Client Visit

February 2023 • Hong Kong

Personalized tour and tasting experience for discerning collectors and high-net-worth clients. The visit included a curated selection of rare and limited-edition spirits chosen specifically for the client's preferences and collection interests.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

This private visit offered a highly personalized experience for a discerning collector, featuring a curated selection of rare and limited-edition spirits from United Spirits' exclusive inventory. The visit was designed to provide an intimate setting where the client could explore premium spirits in depth with expert guidance from a master sommelier. The experience included a private tasting session with a selection of bottles chosen specifically for the client's preferences and collection interests, creating a truly bespoke engagement.

Key Highlights

The visit began with a consultation to understand the client's specific interests, collection focus, and tasting preferences. Based on this information, a personalized selection of 8-10 premium spirits was curated from United Spirits' rare bottle archive. The master sommelier provided detailed insights about each selection, including production history, flavor profile characteristics, and investment potential. The session included a comparative tasting of vintage expressions to demonstrate how aging affects flavor development. The visit also featured a demonstration of proper serving techniques for different spirit categories and personalized recommendations for future acquisitions based on the client's collection goals and preferences.

Personalized Experience

The private visit included several bespoke elements tailored to the client's interests:

  • Custom-curated selection based on the client's collection focus and tasting preferences
  • One-on-one consultation with a master sommelier for in-depth product knowledge
  • Exclusive access to limited editions not available to the general public
  • Personalized acquisition recommendations for collection development
  • Opportunity to request custom bottling of select spirits
  • Dedicated time for questions and discussion without time constraints
  • Follow-up consultation to track collection development and market opportunities

Additional Services

Following the successful visit, United Spirits has expanded its private client services to include several new offerings. The company now provides a "Collection Concierge" service that manages all aspects of a client's spirit collection, including storage, insurance, authentication, and market monitoring. For clients interested in investment opportunities, United Spirits offers a "Market Intelligence" subscription with quarterly reports on market trends and investment opportunities. The company has also introduced a "Tasting Experience" program where clients can host private events for their guests at United Spirits' facilities. Additionally, a new "Custom Blending" service allows clients to create personalized spirit expressions in collaboration with master distillers, resulting in truly unique bottlings that reflect individual taste preferences.
