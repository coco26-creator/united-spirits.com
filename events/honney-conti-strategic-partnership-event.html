Education Event

Vintage Wine Seminar

March 2025 • Hong Kong

In-depth seminar on identifying and appreciating vintage wines from renowned regions. Led by a master sommelier, this session explored the factors that make a wine “vintage” and how to evaluate its quality and aging potential.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

Hosted at a private dining room in Repulse Bay, this seminar welcomed wine collectors and enthusiasts. The sommelier shared insights on how climate, soil, and winemaking techniques influence a vintage’s character. Guests sampled wines from three legendary vintages, learning how to read labels, assess condition, and predict future value.

Key Highlights

The highlight was a “vertical tasting” of Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon from 2018, 2020, and 2023, showcasing how the wine evolves over time. The sommelier also demonstrated how to properly store and decant vintage wines. Guests received a vintage wine guide and a certificate of completion.

Featured Products

The tasting featured these vintages:

  • Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon 2018 - A legendary vintage, full-bodied and complex
  • Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon 2020 - A balanced vintage, with elegant structure
  • Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon 2023 - The current release, vibrant and approachable

Future Initiatives

United Spirits will launch a “Vintage Wine Club,” offering members access to rare vintages, exclusive tastings, and expert advice. The next seminar, “Investing in Wine,” will explore the financial side of wine collecting. A digital “Vintage Wine Tracker” will also be developed, helping users catalog and monitor their collections.
