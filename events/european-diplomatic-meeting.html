Honney Conti XO Presentation

April 2024 • Hong Kong

Exclusive launch event featuring the premium XO cognac collection with master blender insights. The presentation highlighted the exceptional craftsmanship and aging process behind this distinguished brand.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

This exclusive presentation event introduced the Honney Conti XO collection to Hong Kong's premium spirits market. Hosted at United Spirits' flagship tasting room, the event brought together key industry influencers, media representatives, and high-net-worth clients to experience the craftsmanship behind this distinguished brand. The evening featured a masterclass led by the brand's cellar master, who shared insights into the meticulous production process and the careful selection of eaux-de-vie that make this XO exceptional.

Key Highlights

The presentation featured a comprehensive demonstration of the Honney Conti production process, from grape selection to distillation and aging. Attendees experienced a curated tasting journey that highlighted the complex flavor profile of this premium cognac, with special emphasis on how the aging process in French oak barrels contributes to its distinctive character. The cellar master shared exclusive insights about the selection of vintage eaux-de-vie and the art of blending that creates the signature Honney Conti XO profile. The event also included a demonstration of proper serving techniques and ideal food pairings for this premium cognac.

Featured Products

The tasting session highlighted several expressions from the Honney Conti portfolio, including:

  • Honney Conti XO Leather Box — The flagship expression with 10+ years of aging in fine-grained oak
  • Honney Conti XO Royal — A limited edition with enhanced complexity and depth
  • Honney Conti Napoleon VSOP — An introduction to the brand's signature style
  • Honney Conti Mixologist Spirits — Special editions designed for premium cocktail applications

Future Initiatives

Following the successful presentation, United Spirits has committed to organizing quarterly Honney Conti masterclasses that will feature different aspects of cognac production and appreciation. The company will also establish a dedicated Honney Conti showcase area in its flagship tasting room, featuring rotating selections and limited editions. Additionally, a joint initiative with Honney Conti producers will introduce a Hong Kong-exclusive bottling later this year, featuring a unique blend specifically crafted for Asian palates while maintaining the brand's signature quality and character.
