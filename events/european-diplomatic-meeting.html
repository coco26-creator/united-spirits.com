Brand Collaboration

European Diplomatic Meeting — Honney Conti XO Presentation

April 2024 • Hong Kong

Exclusive launch event featuring the Honney Conti XO cognac collection with master blender insights. The presentation highlighted the exceptional craftsmanship and aging process behind this distinguished brand, fostering cultural exchange with European diplomatic representatives.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

This exclusive presentation event introduced the Honney Conti XO collection to Hong Kong's premium spirits market. Hosted at United Spirits' flagship tasting room, the event brought together key industry influencers, media representatives, and European diplomatic envoys. Guests experienced the craftsmanship behind this distinguished brand, while the cellar master shared insights into the meticulous production process and the careful selection of eaux-de-vie that make this XO exceptional.

Key Highlights

The presentation featured a comprehensive demonstration of the Honney Conti production process, from grape selection to distillation and aging. Attendees embarked on a curated tasting journey highlighting the complex flavor profile of this premium cognac. Special emphasis was placed on the aging process in French oak barrels and the art of blending vintage eaux-de-vie that create the brand’s signature style.

  • Masterclass led by Honney Conti’s cellar master
  • Exclusive tasting of XO and limited editions
  • Demonstration of serving techniques and food pairings
  • Dialogue on cultural exchange between European traditions and Asian markets

Featured Products

  • Honney Conti XO Leather Box — Flagship expression with 10+ years aging in fine-grained oak
  • Honney Conti XO Royal — Limited edition with enhanced complexity and depth
  • Honney Conti Napoleon VSOP — A refined introduction to the brand's signature style
  • Honney Conti Mixologist Spirits — Special editions designed for premium cocktail applications

Future Initiatives

Building on this successful collaboration, United Spirits will organize quarterly Honney Conti masterclasses focusing on different aspects of cognac appreciation. Plans include a dedicated Honney Conti showcase area in the flagship tasting room, and the release of a Hong Kong-exclusive bottling crafted specifically for Asian palates while maintaining the brand’s heritage. This initiative strengthens cultural bridges while reinforcing United Spirits' role as a curator of premium global spirits.
