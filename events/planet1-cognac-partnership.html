Planet1 Cognac Partnership

February 2024 • Hong Kong

Strategic alliance signed between United Spirits and Planet1 for Asian market distribution. This partnership establishes a dedicated channel for premium cognac collections throughout Asia.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

This significant partnership announcement established a strategic alliance between United Spirits and Planet1 to distribute premium cognac collections throughout Asia. The signing ceremony, held at United Spirits' Hong Kong headquarters, was attended by executives from both organizations and marked a major milestone in expanding premium cognac access to discerning Asian consumers.

Key Highlights

The partnership signing featured presentations from both companies on their vision for the Asian cognac market. United Spirits' CEO highlighted the growing demand for premium spirits in Asia and the company's commitment to bringing exceptional products to discerning consumers. Planet1's representatives shared insights on their production philosophy and the unique qualities of their cognac collections. The ceremony included a special tasting of Planet1's flagship expressions, allowing attendees to experience firsthand the quality and craftsmanship that will be featured in the partnership. The event concluded with the signing of the distribution agreement and a toast to the successful collaboration.

Strategic Importance

This partnership represents a strategic alignment between United Spirits' extensive Asian distribution network and Planet1's premium cognac portfolio. The collaboration will focus on:

  • Exclusive distribution rights for Planet1 cognacs across major Asian markets
  • Joint marketing initiatives to build brand awareness and appreciation for premium cognac
  • Development of bespoke experiences for high-net-worth individuals through private tastings
  • Creation of limited-edition releases specifically curated for Asian consumer preferences

Future Initiatives

Following the partnership announcement, United Spirits and Planet1 have committed to several joint initiatives. These include the launch of a dedicated Planet1 cognac experience room within United Spirits' flagship location, featuring immersive storytelling about the cognac production process. The companies will also co-host quarterly masterclasses led by Planet1's cellar master, traveling to key Asian markets to engage with consumers and trade partners. Additionally, a special Hong Kong-exclusive bottling is in development, featuring a unique blend that combines Planet1's traditional production methods with flavor profiles tailored to Asian palates, scheduled for release in the fourth quarter of 2024.
