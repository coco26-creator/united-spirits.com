Honney Conti Cask Selection

May 2024 • Hong Kong

Special meeting with producers to select premium casks for Honney Conti collection. The session involved careful tasting and evaluation of potential casks to ensure the highest quality standards for the upcoming release.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

This specialized meeting brought together United Spirits' procurement team with Honney Conti producers to select premium casks for the upcoming Honney Conti collection release. The session involved careful tasting and evaluation of potential casks to ensure the highest quality standards for the premium cognac expressions. The cask selection process is a critical step in maintaining the brand's signature quality and character, and this meeting represented the culmination of months of preparation and barrel monitoring.

Key Highlights

The meeting featured a systematic tasting of 25 potential casks, each evaluated for specific characteristics including color, aroma complexity, flavor profile, and finish. Honney Conti's cellar master led the session, sharing insights about the aging process and how different cask types contribute to the final product. United Spirits' procurement team provided market feedback on consumer preferences in Asia, helping guide the selection toward profiles that would resonate with discerning Asian consumers. The session included detailed documentation of each cask's characteristics and a collaborative decision-making process to select the final barrels that would comprise the upcoming release. The meeting concluded with a signing ceremony formalizing the cask selection and confirming the production schedule for the new release.

Cask Selection Process

The cask selection process followed a rigorous methodology:

  • Initial screening based on analytical data including alcohol content and color intensity
  • First tasting round to eliminate casks with off-notes or inconsistencies
  • Detailed evaluation of remaining casks for specific flavor characteristics
  • Assessment of how individual casks might complement each other in blending
  • Consideration of market preferences for the target release
  • Final selection of casks that met both quality standards and market requirements
  • Documentation of each selected cask's specific characteristics for future reference

Product Development

The selected casks will form the basis of Honney Conti's upcoming XO Royal release, scheduled for Q1 2025. This special edition will feature a blend of eaux-de-vie aged between 12-20 years in fine-grained oak barrels. United Spirits has worked closely with Honney Conti to develop a flavor profile that maintains the brand's signature characteristics while incorporating subtle elements that appeal to Asian palates. The final product will feature enhanced notes of dried fruits and vanilla with a slightly longer finish to complement Asian cuisine pairings. The release will be accompanied by a limited-edition packaging design created in collaboration with a renowned Asian artist, making it a collector's item as well as a premium drinking experience.
