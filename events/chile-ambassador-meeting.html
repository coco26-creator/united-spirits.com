Meeting

Honney Conti Cask Selection

May 2025 • Hong Kong

Special meeting with producers to select premium casks for Honney Conti collection. This exclusive session brought together master blenders, producers, and United Spirits’ leadership to taste and select the finest casks for future releases.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

Held at United Spirits’ private tasting room in Central, this meeting was a critical step in the development of the Honney Conti collection. Master blenders from France presented a selection of casks aged in different woods (French oak, sherry, virgin oak), each imparting unique flavors and aromas. Attendees sampled the spirits directly from the cask, evaluating their potential for future bottlings.

Key Highlights

The highlight was the selection of a rare cask of 25-year-old spirit, matured in sherry wood, for a limited-edition release. The master blender also demonstrated how to “nose” a cask, identifying key aromas that indicate quality and maturity. The session concluded with a unanimous vote on three casks to be bottled under the “Honney Conti XO Royal” label.

Featured Products

The tasting focused on these cask samples:

  • Honney Conti XO (French Oak Cask) - Vanilla, oak, and caramel notes
  • Honney Conti XO (Sherry Cask) - Rich, fruity, with hints of dried fruit and spice
  • Honney Conti XO (Virgin Oak Cask) - Bold and spicy, with strong wood influence

Future Initiatives

United Spirits will host bi-annual “Cask Selection” meetings to ensure a continuous pipeline of premium releases. The next meeting, “The Art of Blending,” will focus on creating new expressions by combining spirits from different casks. A “Cask Tracker” digital platform will also be launched, allowing collectors to follow the journey of their favorite casks from selection to bottling.
