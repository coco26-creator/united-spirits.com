Facility Tour

Wine Vault Experience

November 2022 • Qingdao Free Trade Zone

Immersive tour of our climate-controlled wine vault, showcasing our most prized vintages. This exclusive event for international partners offered a deep dive into our wine portfolio and a guided tasting led by our head sommelier.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

This tour welcomed partners from Europe, Asia, and the Americas to our wine vault in Qingdao. The head sommelier guided guests through the vault, explaining how temperature, humidity, and light are controlled to ensure optimal aging conditions. The tour included a vertical tasting of Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon, showcasing the wine’s evolution over time.

Key Highlights

The highlight was a “blind tasting” of three Cabernet Sauvignon wines from Chile, France, and Australia. Guests were challenged to identify the origin based on flavor and aroma alone. The sommelier also demonstrated how to read a wine label to understand its terroir. The tour concluded with a networking lunch, where partners could discuss future collaborations.

Featured Products

The tasting featured these wines:

  • Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon 2018 - A legendary vintage, full-bodied and complex
  • Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon 2020 - A balanced vintage, with elegant structure
  • Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon 2023 - The current release, vibrant and approachable
  • Corazón de Lolita Red Wine - From Spain’s Rioja region, showcasing elegance and spice

Future Initiatives

United Spirits will host bi-annual “Wine Vault Experiences” for partners and VIP clients. The next event, “The Terroir of Chile,” will focus on the diverse wine regions of Chile. A digital “Wine Vault Explorer” will also be launched, allowing users to browse our collection and learn about each wine’s story.
