Honney Conti XO Presentation

April 2025 • Hong Kong

Exclusive launch event featuring the premium XO cognac collection with master blender insights. Attendees were treated to an intimate presentation by the Honney Conti master blender, who walked guests through the meticulous aging process and unique flavor profile of this exceptional spirit.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

The Honney Conti XO Presentation was an exclusive, invite-only event held at a luxury gallery space in Central Hong Kong. The master blender, flown in from Cognac, France, presented the new XO expression alongside limited-edition bottles from the Royal and Leather Box collections. The event was designed to educate VIP clients and distributors on the craftsmanship behind the brand.

Key Highlights

The master blender conducted a comparative tasting of VSOP, Napoleon, and XO expressions, highlighting the impact of aging and cask selection. Guests were given a behind-the-scenes look at the distillation process through a short documentary. The evening culminated in a live auction of a one-of-a-kind Honney Conti XO bottle, with proceeds donated to a local arts charity.

Featured Products

The presentation focused on these premium expressions:

  • Honney Conti XO Royal - The flagship expression, aged 20+ years
  • Honney Conti XO Leather Box - Collector’s edition with handcrafted leather casing
  • Honney Conti Napoleon VSOP - The entry point to the premium range

Future Initiatives

United Spirits will host a series of “Master Blender Dinners” in key Asian markets (Shanghai, Tokyo, Singapore) throughout 2025. These intimate events will pair Honney Conti expressions with gourmet cuisine. Additionally, a digital “Cognac Academy” will be launched, offering virtual tastings and educational content for enthusiasts and trade partners.
