Brandy Tasting 101

April 2024 • Hong Kong

Introductory workshop on Cognac and Armagnac production, aging, and evaluation techniques. Led by a brandy specialist, the session covered the entire production process and proper tasting methodology for premium brandies.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

This introductory brandy tasting workshop provided foundational knowledge about Cognac and Armagnac production, aging, and evaluation for both beginners and intermediate enthusiasts. Led by a certified brandy specialist with over 15 years of experience, the session covered the entire production process from grape selection to distillation and aging in oak barrels. Participants learned the proper technique for brandy tasting, including visual assessment, aroma evaluation, and flavor analysis, through a guided tasting of six premium brandies representing different styles and age classifications.

Key Highlights

The workshop featured a comprehensive overview of the brandy production process, with special emphasis on the differences between Cognac and Armagnac. Participants learned about the grape varieties used, the double distillation process for Cognac, and the single distillation method for Armagnac. The session included a detailed explanation of the aging process in oak barrels and how different classifications (VS, VSOP, XO) are determined. The brandy specialist demonstrated the proper tasting technique using tulip-shaped glasses and guided participants through a systematic evaluation of appearance, aroma, and flavor characteristics. The workshop also covered common misconceptions about brandy and how to properly store and serve premium brandies.

Featured Products

The tasting session highlighted six premium brandies from United Spirits' portfolio, including:

  • Honney Conti VSOP - An excellent introduction to the brandy category with balanced oak influence
  • Honney Conti Napoleon VSOP - Showcasing greater complexity and depth with extended aging
  • Honney Conti XO - Representing the pinnacle of brandy craftsmanship with 10+ years of aging
  • Honney Conti XO Royal - A limited edition with enhanced complexity and distinctive character
  • Planet1 Cognac VSOP - Demonstrating the house style of this premium producer
  • Planet1 Cognac XO - Highlighting the impact of extended aging in fine-grained oak barrels

Learning Outcomes

Participants gained the ability to distinguish between different brandy styles and classifications through sensory evaluation. They learned the proper terminology for describing brandy characteristics and how to identify key flavor markers that indicate production methods and aging duration. The workshop provided practical knowledge for selecting brandies based on personal preferences and for different occasions. Attendees received a detailed tasting guide with visual references for color assessment, aroma identification, and flavor profiling. The session concluded with a Q&A where the specialist addressed specific questions about brandy production, collecting, and investment potential, providing valuable insights for both casual enthusiasts and serious collectors.
