Diplomatic

Chile Ambassador Visit

May 2024 • Hong Kong

Formal visit by the Chilean Ambassador to United Spirits' Hong Kong headquarters. The visit included discussions on expanding Chilean wine exports to Asia and exploring opportunities for joint marketing initiatives.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

This formal diplomatic visit by the Chilean Ambassador to United Spirits' Hong Kong headquarters was part of a cultural diplomacy initiative to strengthen trade relations between Chile and Hong Kong in the premium spirits sector. The visit focused on exploring opportunities for Chilean wine producers in the Asian market and establishing stronger connections between Chilean winemakers and Asian distributors.

Key Highlights

The Ambassador was given a comprehensive tour of United Spirits' facilities, including the tasting room and climate-controlled warehouse. The visit featured a curated selection of premium Chilean wines from United Spirits' portfolio, with special emphasis on the Anderra collection. United Spirits' procurement team presented market insights on consumer preferences in Asia and strategies for expanding Chilean wine presence in the region. The Ambassador shared updates on Chile's viticulture developments and sustainability initiatives in wine production. The discussion also covered potential collaborations for cultural exchange events that would promote Chilean wines in Asian markets.

Featured Products

The tasting session highlighted several premium Chilean wines from United Spirits' collection, including:

  • Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon 2023 - Representing Chile's premier red wine production
  • Anderra Merlot 2023 - Showcasing Chile's versatility in red varietals
  • Anderra Reserva Carmenere 2021 - Highlighting Chile's signature grape variety
  • El Rey Cisne 2022 Red - Demonstrating innovative winemaking techniques in Chile

Future Initiatives

Following the successful visit, United Spirits has committed to organizing a dedicated Chilean Wine Month in Hong Kong, featuring special tastings, masterclasses, and promotional events. The company will also establish a Chilean Wine Showcase area in its flagship tasting room, featuring rotating selections from different Chilean regions. Additionally, United Spirits will collaborate with the Chilean Trade Office to develop educational materials for Asian consumers about Chilean wine regions and varietals, with the goal of increasing awareness and appreciation for Chilean wines in the Asian market. A joint delegation visit to Chilean vineyards is also planned for Q4 2024 to strengthen relationships with producers.
