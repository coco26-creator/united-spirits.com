Diplomatic Visit

Spanish Trade Delegation

March 2025 • Hong Kong

Meeting with Spanish trade representatives to explore premium spirits opportunities. The delegation toured United Spirits’ facilities and sampled a curated selection of Spanish wines and brandies.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

The Spanish Trade Delegation, led by the Commercial Counselor from the Spanish Consulate, visited United Spirits to explore potential collaborations in the premium spirits market. The day included a tour of the temperature-controlled warehouse, a private tasting of Spanish wines and brandies, and a roundtable discussion on market trends and consumer preferences in Asia.

Key Highlights

The delegation was particularly impressed by the “Wine Vault,” a climate-controlled archive housing rare vintages. A tasting of Honney Conti VSOP and XO expressions was conducted, showcasing the brand’s French heritage. The highlight was a presentation on United Spirits’ distribution network and marketing capabilities in Greater China. The visit concluded with a commitment to explore co-branding opportunities.

Featured Products

The tasting focused on these Spanish gems:

  • Honney Conti VSOP - Smooth and approachable, perfect for cocktails
  • Honney Conti XO Royal - Rich and complex, aged in French oak
  • Corazón de Lolita Red Wine - Vibrant and fruit-forward, with notes of cherry and spice

Future Initiatives

Following the visit, United Spirits will organize a “Spanish Spirits Week” in Q4 2025, featuring tastings, dinners, and retail promotions across Hong Kong. A co-branded “Spain & Cognac” pop-up experience is planned for Shanghai and Singapore. Both parties will also collaborate on a digital campaign highlighting the craftsmanship behind Spanish wines and French brandies.
