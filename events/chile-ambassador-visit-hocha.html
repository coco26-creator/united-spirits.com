Diplomatic Visit

Chile Embassy Wine Tasting

April 2025 • Chilean Embassy, Hong Kong

Exclusive tasting event hosted at the Chilean Embassy featuring premium Chilean wines. Guests enjoyed a curated selection of reds and whites from award-winning vineyards, paired with gourmet Chilean cuisine.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

Hosted by the Chilean Ambassador, this intimate wine tasting brought together diplomats, trade officials, and key distributors. The event showcased Chile’s diverse wine regions, from the cool-climate valleys of Casablanca to the sun-drenched slopes of Colchagua. United Spirits’ sommelier guided guests through the tasting, highlighting the unique terroir of each wine.

Key Highlights

The highlight was a vertical tasting of Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon, comparing vintages from 2020 to 2023. Guests also enjoyed a rare tasting of Corazón de Lolita’s limited-edition white wine. The Ambassador gave a heartfelt toast, emphasizing the cultural and economic ties between Chile and Hong Kong. The event concluded with a signing of a new distribution agreement.

Featured Products

The tasting featured these Chilean stars:

  • Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon 2023 - Bold and structured, with notes of blackberry and spice
  • Corazón de Lolita White Wine - Crisp and aromatic, with hints of citrus and white flowers
  • Anderra Reserva Sauvignon Blanc 2024 - Refreshing and vibrant, perfect for Hong Kong’s humid climate

Future Initiatives

Following the event, United Spirits will launch a “Chilean Wine Month” in June 2025, featuring in-store tastings and special promotions. A documentary series on Chilean winemaking, produced in collaboration with the Chilean Trade Commission, will be released on social media. Plans are also underway for a reciprocal visit by United Spirits’ CEO to Chile in 2026.
