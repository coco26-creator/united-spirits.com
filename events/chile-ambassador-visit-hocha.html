Diplomatic Visit

Chile Ambassador — Hocha Winery Visit

June 2023 • Qingdao

The Chilean Ambassador’s delegation toured Hocha Winery, engaging with production teams and exploring sustainable viticulture methods in China’s coastal wine region.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

The tour highlighted China–Chile cooperation in winemaking, with Hocha presenting research on coastal terroirs and the Ambassador emphasizing bilateral trade in premium wines.

Key Highlights

  • Winery tour focusing on organic farming practices
  • Roundtable discussion on Sino-Chilean wine trade
  • Private tasting of flagship vintages

Future Initiatives

Both parties agreed to develop a Chile–China winemaking exchange program, bringing Chilean experts to local vineyards and strengthening export channels.
