Facility Tour

VIP Warehouse Tour

May 2023 • Qingdao Free Trade Zone

Behind-the-scenes access to our temperature-controlled warehouse facilities for VIP clients. This exclusive tour, hosted for the Chilean Ambassador and his delegation, showcased our state-of-the-art logistics and quality control processes.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

The VIP Warehouse Tour offered an intimate look at United Spirits’ logistics hub in the Qingdao Free Trade Zone. The Chilean delegation, led by the Ambassador, was given a comprehensive tour of the facility, including the receiving bay, temperature-controlled storage zones, and the rare bottle archive. The warehouse manager explained how technology and rigorous protocols ensure the perfect storage conditions for every bottle.

Key Highlights

The highlight was a visit to the “Climate-Controlled Vault,” where premium wines and spirits are stored at precise temperatures and humidity levels. The Ambassador was particularly impressed by the automated inventory system and the security measures in place. The tour concluded with a private tasting of Anderra Reserva wines, highlighting the seamless journey from warehouse to glass.

Featured Products

The tasting featured these Chilean wines:

  • Anderra Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon 2023 - The flagship wine, full-bodied and elegant
  • Anderra Reserva Sauvignon Blanc 2024 - Crisp and aromatic, perfect for Hong Kong’s climate
  • Corazón de Lolita Red Wine - A vibrant, fruit-forward expression

Future Initiatives

United Spirits will offer monthly “Warehouse Tours” for VIP clients and distributors. The next tour, “The Art of Blending,” will include a visit to the blending room and a hands-on blending workshop. A digital “Warehouse 360° Tour” will also be launched, allowing users to explore the facility virtually.
