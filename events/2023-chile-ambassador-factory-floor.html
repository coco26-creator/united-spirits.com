VIP Warehouse Tour

May 2023 • Hong Kong

Behind-the-scenes access to United Spirits' temperature-controlled warehouse facilities for VIP clients. The tour highlighted the company's commitment to preserving the quality and integrity of rare and vintage spirits through meticulous storage practices.

← Back to Events

Event Overview

This exclusive warehouse tour provided VIP clients with behind-the-scenes access to United Spirits' state-of-the-art storage facilities. The event was designed for discerning collectors and high-net-worth clients who value the importance of proper storage conditions for premium spirits. Participants learned about the specialized climate control systems that maintain optimal conditions for aging premium spirits, including precise temperature and humidity regulation that preserves the quality and integrity of rare and vintage collections.

Key Highlights

The tour featured an in-depth explanation of the warehouse's advanced climate control technology, with demonstrations of the monitoring systems that maintain precise environmental conditions. Participants observed how different spirit categories require specific storage parameters to optimize aging or preserve vintage character. The tour guide explained the security protocols in place for protecting high-value collections, including biometric access controls and 24/7 surveillance. The session included a demonstration of the company's inventory management system, which tracks each bottle's provenance and storage history. The tour concluded with a private tasting session featuring select spirits that had been stored under optimal conditions, allowing participants to directly experience the difference proper storage makes in quality preservation.

Facility Features

The tour highlighted several key aspects of United Spirits' warehouse facilities:

  • Temperature-controlled zones ranging from 8°C to 22°C for different spirit categories
  • Humidity regulation systems maintaining 60-70% relative humidity for optimal cork preservation
  • UV-filtered lighting to prevent light damage to sensitive spirits
  • Specialized racking systems designed to minimize vibration and movement
  • Advanced security systems including biometric access controls and 24/7 monitoring
  • Dedicated areas for rare and vintage collections with enhanced security measures

Client Benefits

Following the tour, VIP clients gained access to United Spirits' premium storage services for their personal collections. The company offers bespoke storage solutions with regular condition reports and inventory management. Clients can schedule private viewing appointments to examine their collections or select bottles for special occasions. United Spirits also provides authentication services for rare bottles and documentation of storage history for investment purposes. The tour introduced clients to the company's concierge service, which handles bottle retrieval, delivery, and even coordinates private tasting events featuring their stored collections. This service has proven particularly valuable for collectors who maintain significant spirit investments but lack proper storage facilities at home.
