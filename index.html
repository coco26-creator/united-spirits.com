From Your Winery to Asia — Customize, Package, and Grow with Us

We transform global wines and spirits into Asian success stories — from concept to shelf, fully managed through our integrated Qingdao Free Trade Zone and Hong Kong platform.

What We Offer

At United Spirits, we connect international producers with Asia’s fastest-growing markets. Our Customization Hub in Qingdao Free Trade Zone and logistics base in Hong Kong give producers a turnkey route into Asia.

  • Customs & bonded management — full documentation, clearance, and tax-optimized operations.
  • Packaging & bottling — transforming bulk shipments into premium retail products.
  • Design & branding — adapting your storytelling for Asian consumers.
  • Storage & logistics — bonded warehousing and seamless regional exports.
  • Distribution — access to retail, e-commerce, and on-trade channels across China, Japan, Korea, ASEAN, and Ghana.

You produce the wine — we handle the rest.

Where We Operate

Headquartered in Hong Kong — a global hub for trade, finance, and logistics — and operating from the Qingdao Free Trade Zone, we leverage Asia’s most advanced supply-chain ecosystem to source, customize, and distribute premium wines and spirits worldwide, including Ghana.

  • End-to-end production oversight with ISO-certified partners.
  • Fast turnaround & scalable output.
  • Private-label & co-branding programs.
  • Sustainable practices & quality assurance.
