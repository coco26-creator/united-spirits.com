Our Supply Chain Advantage

Leveraging Hong Kong as a global platform and China’s world-class manufacturing ecosystem to source, produce, and distribute premium spirits worldwide.

Hong Kong as a Global Platform

United Spirits is headquartered in Hong Kong, a trusted international hub for trade, finance, and logistics. This strategic location enables seamless access to global markets with efficient customs, strong legal frameworks, and world-class infrastructure.

China’s Complete Supply Chain

Our production facilities are based in mainland China, where we benefit from one of the most advanced and comprehensive supply chains in the world. From sourcing raw materials to bottling, packaging, and labeling — we maintain full control over quality and efficiency.

  • End-to-end production oversight
  • Fast turnaround & scalable output
  • Custom bottling, co-branding & private label
  • ISO-certified partners and sustainable practices

Global Sourcing, Worldwide Distribution

We source premium wines, whiskeys, and brandies from producers across Europe, the Americas, and Asia — then distribute through Hong Kong to clients in North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and beyond.
