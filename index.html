The Process

From your cellar to Asia's shelves.
Five steps.

1
Pickup & Transport

ZG Zhong Gang Logistics coordinates door-to-door pickup from your winery or distillery — documentation, freight, and insurance fully managed.

2
Customs & Bonded Entry

Our bonded team handles import declaration, CIQ inspection, and compliance. Products remain duty-free inside our Qingdao FTZ facility until sold.

3
Customization & Packaging

We design and produce labels, boxes, and packaging adapted for the Asian market — positioning your brand for local consumers and retail environments.

4
Bonded Storage

Temperature-controlled bonded warehouses with real-time stock monitoring. Your inventory is secure, compliant, and ready to move when orders arrive.

5
Distribution & Sales

Our sales team manages regional distribution through retail chains, e-commerce platforms, and hospitality partners across 29 countries in Asia and Africa.
