About Us

Founded in 2015, United Spirits has grown from a passion for exceptional beverages into a global platform connecting producers and consumers worldwide.

Who We Are

Founded in 2015 in Hong Kong, United Spirits began with a vision: to connect world-class wine and spirit producers with Asia’s fastest-growing markets. What started as a curated import business has evolved into a full-service platform — combining customs expertise, packaging innovation, and distribution power inside China’s Qingdao Free Trade Zone.

Today, we don’t just select premium bottles — we create them. From concept to bottling, we design, produce, and distribute our own labels while also offering bespoke manufacturing services for global partners through our facility in the Qingdao Free Trade Zone.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class spirits that celebrate heritage, craftsmanship, and innovation. We are committed to building lasting relationships with our partners and clients, ensuring every bottle we distribute represents excellence and authenticity. Through careful curation and unwavering dedication to quality, we strive to elevate the appreciation of premium spirits across Asia and beyond.

What We Do

We specialize in the import, customization, bottling, branding, and distribution of premium wines and spirits across Asia.

In addition, we help global producers launch their brands in China and beyond — and empower entrepreneurs to build their own labels through our BYOB (Build Your Own Brand) program.

As a manufacturer, we offer end-to-end production services, from sourcing raw materials to final packaging, ensuring quality and efficiency at every step.

How We Do It

From concept to shelf, everything happens within the Qingdao Free Trade Zone, where we manage:

  • Full customs & bonded logistics
  • Premium packaging & co-branding
  • Distribution across China, Japan, Korea & ASEAN
  • End-to-end project management
