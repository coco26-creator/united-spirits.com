About United Spirits

United Spirits is a Hong Kong–based importer and distributor of premium wines, whiskeys, and brandies. We collaborate with leading producers and connect them to discerning markets across Asia and Africa.

Our Story

Founded in Hong Kong, United Spirits has built a strong reputation for excellence in sourcing, brand management, and distribution. Our mission is to make exceptional beverages accessible to consumers through trusted trade partners.

From boutique vineyards to established distilleries, we proudly represent producers who share our commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and authenticity.

Our Mission

To connect exceptional producers with discerning consumers through transparent, reliable, and sustainable partnerships. We bridge continents — from Asia to Africa — ensuring premium spirits reach the right audiences with integrity and style.
