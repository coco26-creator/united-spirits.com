Who We Are

United Spirits Holding is a full-service manufacturing and customization hub based in Qingdao Free Trade Zone. We help global wineries and distilleries transform their wines and spirits for Asian and international success.

As both a bonded manufacturer and logistics partner, we handle everything — from customs clearance, bottling, and label adaptation to storage and distribution. Our integrated setup allows seamless entry into high-potential markets while ensuring compliance and efficiency.

We’re more than a service provider — we’re your strategic partner in building sustainable brand growth across Asia and Africa.
