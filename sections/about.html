About Us

Headquartered in Hong Kong, Spirits United Supply Chain Co., Limited. operates distilleries and wineries in Europe and Asia, crafting premium beverages for the rapidly expanding markets of Asia and Africa.

We curate award-winning wines, refined whiskeys, and exceptional brandies from storied houses around the world. We partner with distributors who share our obsession for quality, craft, and character.

Who We Are

We began as a bridge between world-class distilleries and emerging Asian markets. Today, we are proud to operate as both a manufacturer and distributor — crafting, bottling, and exporting premium wines, whiskeys, and brandies.

Based in the Qingdao Free Trade Zone, we oversee production, blending, bottling, export logistics, and brand development with precision and care.

Our Mission

To elevate global spirits through quality, authenticity, and collaboration — connecting exceptional distributor and suppliers across the World.
